for taking a look at our dataset.

As part of our course "Three-Dimensional Foundations in Media Space" at HfG Schwäbisch Gmünd, we deliberately chose the topic "Missing Migrants 2014-2023." Through our data visualization, we aimed to highlight the numerous global conflict zones that lead to displacement and migration, and to bring attention to the fate of missing migrants. Our goal was to raise awareness of the often overlooked tragedies and the humanitarian dimension of this global issue.

During our research into individual stories that we integrated into our data visualization, we came across the humanitarian organizations Amnesty International and Sea-Watch. As these organizations play a crucial role in supporting migrants, we would like to link them here to give you the opportunity to donate and help.
